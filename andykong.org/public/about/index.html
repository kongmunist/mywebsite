Howdy!
I'm Andy Kong

I'm currently studying at ETH Zurich in the Direct Doctorate program, a joint Master's + PhD track in the Department of Computer Science.

My research interests include augmented human perception, computational photography, personal informatics for health, and increasing the human-computer communication rate.
I love to think and work on new things, and my overarching goal is to make thinking and learning easier for everyone. I'm interested in applying intuitive interfaces, memory extension, AI-human collaboration, and human quantification to that end.

You're sitting on my website! I built it to document my personal and research work, and to store my writing, thoughts, and project logs in my blog. If you find any cool topics you think I'd find interesting, I'd love to hear about it! Email me or find me on Twitter!
