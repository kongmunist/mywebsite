Hello, I'm Andy

I live in San Francisco. I like building sensors to better understand the mind and body.

I've spent much of my light researching human-computer interaction + biosensing (CMU, ETH Zurich) and building quantum physics hardware (Open Quantum). Nowadays I'm running Chargerless, a consumer company building perpetual devices.

In my free time I enjoy new experiences, training my senses, seeing things I've never seen before, challenging what I believe is possible, and inventing sufficiently advanced technology.
